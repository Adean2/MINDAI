About Me

Hi, my name is Aidan Engler. I was born and raised in Raleigh, North Carolina. Digital Art is my passion. When I was younger, building games were my favorite. I would create enormous structures to not only impress my friends but to also test myself and the game’s limits. High school my favorite classes were the ones that used 3D modeling programs. Art and computer science classes made me feel unique. Today, I’m currently a senior at UNCW finishing my Digital Arts degree. I am primarily focused on designing 3D models and websites.
